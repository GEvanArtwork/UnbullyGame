Play Now!

Unbully is a platform and exploration game where you control Tales, a small dragon bullied by the other dragons.

Help him cross the forest, collect lost eggs, and forge his own path. As Tales overcomes challenges, he earns the trust of those who once hurt him, and they begin to change by supporting him with positive attitudes.

A tale of adventure, respect, friendship, and the courage to be different.

About the Project

Unbully is part of a research project that explores fantasy video games as a tool to help young people reflect on hate speech (without feeling like a class lesson).

Real players, real impact.
